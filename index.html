Honey boosts immunity, improves digestion, provides natural energy, and has antibacterial properties.

Yes. Consuming 1–2 teaspoons daily helps with metabolism, immunity, and overall wellness.

Is honey good for skin and hair?

Absolutely. Honey moisturizes skin, fights acne, and nourishes hair when used in masks.

Can honey replace sugar?

Yes. Honey is a healthier natural sweetener compared to refined sugar.
